About

I'm an Electrical Engineering undergraduate at the University of Moratuwa, specialising in power electronics and control systems.

This portfolio documents my technical work — design processes, full mathematical derivations, simulation results, and source code. I believe that engineering insight lives in the derivation, not just the simulation output.

Alongside my EE core, I'm building software skills in Java and Spring Boot, working toward projects that bridge embedded firmware and backend systems.

Education

University of Moratuwa B.Sc. Engineering (Electrical) — In Progress Specialisation: Power Electronics & Energy Systems

Technical Focus

Power Converters

Buck, boost, flyback, full-bridge topologies. CCM/DCM analysis. Magnetics design.

Control Theory

State-space averaging, small-signal models, PI/PID design, digital implementation via Tustin transform.

Simulation Tools

QSpice for circuit-level simulation, MATLAB/Simulink for system-level modelling and control design.

Software Development

Java, Spring Boot, REST APIs, JPA. Interested in embedded firmware and IoT system architecture.
